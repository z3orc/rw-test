This is just an easier way of downloading a specific version of the minecraft-server jar. It supports downloading JARs from Mojang, Paper and Purpur.

Just use https://mcdl.z3orc.com/*flavour*/*version*

flavour would be either vanilla, paper or purpur (based on which server-jar you prefer.) and version would be the minecraft version, 1.18.1 - 1.17.1 - etc.
